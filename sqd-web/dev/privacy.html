Privacy Policy

A. Squidler Website
1. Changes to the privacy policy - We will notify the users via a popup when they next visit the site
2. Data we collect about you - None
3. Data we store in your browser - Small pieces of text in your browser's local storage to improve your experience, reduce your data consumption and to ensure we are able to deliver important notifications to you
4. Data security - We use secure SSL/TLS connections to transfer all data

B. Squidler App
1. Changes to the privacy policy - We will notify the users via a popup when they next load the app
2. Data we collect about you - Email address when you sign-up, profile data you optionally provide (name, bio, location and avatar) and your current ip address
3. Your data that we make publicly available - Your profile (if you choose to create one) i.e. name, bio, location and avatar
4. Data security - We use secure SSL/TLS connections to transfer all data. We do not store your email address as plain text, so that in the event of a security breach your contact information is still private
5. Sharing your data with other organisations - The only time we will share any of your data is to to comply with requests from law enforcement agencies
6. Data we store on your phone - Small pieces of text in your apps local storage to improve your experience, reduce your data consumption and to ensure we are able to deliver important notifications to you. We also temporarily store copies of images downloaded through the app to reduce your data consumption - these can be cleared from within the settings
7. In app purchases - We use Apple and Google payment services to securely make transactions. They share purchase receipt information with us to enable us to verify transactions which are then stored on our servers. We do not at any time have access to your credit/debit card information
