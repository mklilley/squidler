Squidler

Turns messaging into a game
Challenge your friends in a fun and unique way by sending them a locked message which they can only unlock by answering a question you set

How it works:

1. Craft your message
send text, image, animation, or video
2. Lock it with a question
try something challenging that your friend will find fun
3. Share it via your favourite social or messaging app
no one can see your Squidle unless they answer your question correctly

To get started:

You don't need the app to play

Just tap on a Squidle e.g. squidler.com/ImASquidle
to start Squidling

Download our FREE app to create Squidles of your own
